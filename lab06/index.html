About Us

Since its founding on April 2nd, 2019, Steven's Veterinary Hospital has offered a variety of high-quality pet services to over 1,500 clients in the Ottawa-Gatineau region.

The team, from the receptionists to the veterinarians, attributes their award-winning services to the charismatic clients and cute animals they encounter.

A wide range of services are offered at competitive prices. Since the very beginning, Steven's Veterinary Hospital has been committed to offering excellent services at a low cost.

Microchipping

  • Price: $50
  • Duration: 5 - 15 minutes

Protecting your pet from getting lost or being stolen is very important.

An implanted microchip with your pet's unique identification is permanent and can be the difference between lost and found.

Pharmacy

  • Price Range: $10 - $100
  • Duration: 5 - 30 minutes

There may be a need for your pet to be on medication.

If there is a medication that your pet requires that we don't have in stock, we can have it ordered in at your convenience. If there is an ongoing need for medicine, the prescription may be refilled with a simple phone call.

Preventive care

  • Price: $35
  • Duration: 30 - 90 minutes

It is always better to maintain good health than trying to restore health after an illness.

A few cornerstones to good health include proper nutrition and exercise, control of internal and external parasites, maintaining good dental health and prevention of infectious diseases.

Surgery

  • Price Range: $500 - $1,500
  • Duration: 30 - 120 minutes

The first, and hopefully only, surgery your pet will ever need is the spay or neuter procedure.

Some common procedures include lump removals, biopsies, the removal of bladder stones, and abdominal surgery to remove foreign body obstructions.

Vaccination

  • Price Range: $5 - $10
  • Duration: 5 - 15 minutes

Vaccination is important for your pet‚Äôs health.

We use vaccines with the longest possible duration of immunity to ensure your pet will receive the least number of vaccinations over his or her lifetime. Most of the vaccines we use have a three-year duration of immunity.
