About Us

Since its founding on April 2nd, 2019, Steven's Veterinary Hospital has offered a variety of high-quality pet services to over 1,500 clients in the Ottawa-Gatineau region.

The team, from the receptionists to the veterinarians, attribute their award-winning services to the charismatic clients and cute animals they encounter.

Having won the Capital Region Best Critter Caregivers Award in 2020, the team has been expanding to accomodate for more appointment slots!
